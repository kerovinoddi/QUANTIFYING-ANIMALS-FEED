WHY QUANTIFY YOUR ANIMALS FEED?



This web page have been developed to help eradicate death of animals over this years the rate at which the animals have been dying is alarming. Due to this reason I sees it wise to come up with measures that will help to conserve our livestock and wild animals.


The ideology of quantifying animals feed is good and by this just like we budget for our selves we can budget for our wild animals since they earns us income just like domestic animals does.


For good health animal at home their movements should be restricteted that is their grazing land at home should be devided into paddocks. they should graze rotationally. To do this the following tools are necessary :asckle or apanga.
